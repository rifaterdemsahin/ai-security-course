Learning Objectives

By the end of this lesson, you will be able to:

Core Defense Strategies

Adversarial Training
The Immunity Principle: Turn AI's weakness into strength.
  • "Vaccinating" your model against attacks
  • Generating adversarial examples for training
  • Trade-offs between robustness and accuracy
Input Sanitization
The Gatekeeper Strategy: Simple but powerful protection.
  • Input validation techniques
  • Statistical outlier detection
  • Preprocessing pipelines
Differential Privacy
Privacy by Design: Insights + Privacy simultaneously.
  • Mathematical privacy foundation
  • Noise injection for protection
  • Privacy-utility trade-offs
